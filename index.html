Our Legacy of Stone

Founded in 2004, Shayam Marble operates with a singular vision: to bring the earth's natural beauty into everyday living spaces. Our sprawling selection encompasses the finest Italian Marbles, dense Granites, and ethereal Onyx series. We believe each slab is a piece of art.

Our Mission

To provide architects and homeowners with exceptional stone materials coupled with unparalleled service.

Our Vision

To be the nation's most trusted name in luxury natural stone, pioneering sustainable quarrying and processing.
