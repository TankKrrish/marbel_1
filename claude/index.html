About This Stone

Statuario Bianco is among the most prestigious and sought-after marbles in the world. Its distinctive characteristic lies in the bold, dramatic veining that cuts across an almost luminous white background — a visual drama that has captivated architects and designers for millennia.

Quarried from the renowned Apuan Alps in the Carrara region of Tuscany, Italy, this marble has been used in some of history's most iconic structures. Its deep veins, ranging from soft grey to near black, create unique, unrepeatable patterns that make every slab a one-of-a-kind work of art.

Ideal for flooring, wall cladding, countertops, bathroom vanities, and statement feature walls. The polished finish enhances the natural lustre of the stone, while a honed finish provides a matte, more contemporary look. Each batch is carefully inspected by our quality team to ensure consistent coloring, thickness, and surface quality.

Our Journey

Crafting Excellence Since 2004

Founded in Ahmedabad, Gujarat, Shayam Marble began as a small showroom with a passionate vision — to make premium natural stone accessible to every Indian home.

Today, we serve thousands of clients across India, from individual homeowners to luxury hotels, corporate offices, and renowned architects. Our 40,000 sq. ft. warehouse stocks over 500 varieties of marble, granite, sandstone, and exotic stones sourced from 18 countries.

Our team of 80+ skilled professionals — including stone specialists, designers, and craftsmen — ensures that every project is handled with care, precision, and the passion that has defined Shayam Marble for two decades.
